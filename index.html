Roadmap

2025 — Aug–Sep • Hardening & Mainnet

  • Home: Hero + How to Buy; routes for /explore, /creators, /purchases.
  • Purchase: SOL price → Phantom confirm → decrypt & reveal → saved to My Purchases.
  • Reliability: encryption, clear errors, monitoring & basic analytics.
  • Targets: <2 min checkout, ≥99% unlock, zero console errors.

2025 — Oct–Dec • Public Beta

  • Creators: onboarding, pricing, draft/publish, version notes.
  • Quality: ratings & reviews.
  • Purchases: search/filter, re-reveal, copy, tips.
  • Economics: marketplace fee & creator payouts.
  • Launch: 20 seed creators; weekly “Top Prompts”.
  • Targets: 100+ prompts, 50+ purchases, refunds <3%, global load <200ms.

2026 — Q1 • Growth Foundations

  • Collections/bundles, coupons, featured placements.
  • Creator analytics v1 (views → conversion → sales → payouts).
  • Search & tags; simple report flow.
  • Targets: 500 WAU, 200 monthly purchases, NPS ≥ 40.

2026 — Q2 • Teams & API

  • Team seats (shared purchases across wallets/emails).
  • API & webhooks; affiliates/referrals with tracked payouts.
  • Targets: 1K WAU, 400 monthly purchases, 20 org teams, 10+ API users.

2026 — Q3 • Trust & Performance

  • Moderation v2; creator verification badges.
  • Recommendations (collaborative + content tags); start localization.
  • Targets: repeat purchase ≥ 30%; moderation SLA <24h.

2026 — Q4 • Scale & Compliance

  • Tax/GST & invoices; creator earnings dashboard + exports.
  • Multi-currency display (USD/EUR; transact in SOL); advanced bundles; subscriptions pilot.
  • Targets: 2K WAU, 1K monthly purchases, refunds <2%, uptime 99.95%.
